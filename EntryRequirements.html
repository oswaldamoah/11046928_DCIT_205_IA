Entry Requirements

For Mathematical Sciences

Bachelor of Science in Mathematical Sciences encompasses the following:
  • Actuarial Science
  • Biomathematics
  • Computer Science
  • Mathematics
  • Statistics
  • Physics
Core requirements include credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies. Electives mandate credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any other two subjects.

For Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology requires credit passes in English, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies as core subjects. Electives necessitate credit passes in any three subjects.. This comprehensive program equips students with a versatile skill set spanning mathematical disciplines, preparing them for diverse career paths in areas such as actuarial science, biomathematics, computer science, mathematics, statistics, and physics.
