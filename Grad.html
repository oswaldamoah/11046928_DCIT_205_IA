Graduate Programmes

The Graduate Department of Computer Science in the University og Ghana offers a range of programmes designed to enhance the academic and professional prospects of its students.

These include the Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science, the Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science, and the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science.

Pursuing these computer science degrees equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to tackle complex problems and innovate in the ever-evolving technological landscape.
