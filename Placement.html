Placement

A student can be placed into the following programs:

  • Computer Science Single Major
  • Computer Science Combined Major with Mathematics/Statistics
  • Mathematics Major and Minor in Computer Science
  • Information Technology Single Major
  • Physics/Chemistry with Information Tecnology

Requirements for Computer Science Single and Combined Major

Single Major:

A Minimum of C (Pass) in MATH121, MATH123, MATH122 and MATH126 and a minimum of B+ in DCIT101, DCIT103, DCIT102 and DCIT104.


Combined Major:

A Minimum of C (Pass) in MATH121, MATH123, MATH122 and MATH126 and a minimum of B in DCIT101, DCIT103, DCIT102 and DCIT104.
